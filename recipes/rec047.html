Turkey Enchilada Stuffed Peppers

With Black Beans, Sweetcorn & Lime Yoghurt

Ingredients

  • 300 g Turkey Mince
  • 2 each Red Pepper
  • 160 g Black Beans
  • 100 g Sweetcorn
  • 200 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 30 g Cheddar
  • 100 g Greek Yoghurt
  • 1 each Lime
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Chipotle Paste
  • 1 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

Method

  1. 1
    Heat the oven to 200°C fan, halve the peppers lengthways and remove the seeds.
  2. 2
    Place the pepper halves cut-side up in a baking dish, add a splash of water and bake for 10 minutes.
  3. 3
    Meanwhile, heat the rapeseed oil in a frying pan and cook the turkey mince and diced red onion for six minutes, breaking up the mince.
  4. 4
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, smoked paprika, cumin and chipotle paste and cook for one minute.
  5. 5
    Add the chopped tomatoes, drained black beans and sweetcorn and simmer for five minutes until thick.
  6. 6
    Spoon the filling into the pepper halves, scatter over the cheddar and bake for 12 to 15 minutes until bubbling.
  7. 7
    Mix the Greek yoghurt with the lime zest and juice.
  8. 8
    Serve two stuffed pepper halves per person with the lime yoghurt spooned over the top.
