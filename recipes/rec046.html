Moroccan Chicken & Apricot Tagine

With Chickpeas, Carrots & Fragrant Spices

Ingredients

  • 500 g Chicken Breast
  • 240 g Chickpeas
  • 80 g Dried Apricots
  • 240 g Wholewheat Couscous
  • 400 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 300 g Carrots
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 2 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Ground Coriander
  • 0.5 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 0.5 tsp Turmeric
  • 300 ml Low-Salt Chicken Stock
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 15 g Fresh Coriander
  • 1 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

Method

  1. 1
    Cut the chicken into large chunks, slice the carrots and onion and halve the dried apricots.
  2. 2
    Heat the rapeseed oil in a large lidded pan and brown the chicken for four minutes.
  3. 3
    Add the onion and carrots and cook for five minutes, stirring regularly.
  4. 4
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, cumin, coriander, cinnamon and turmeric for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. 5
    Add the chopped tomatoes, low-salt stock, drained chickpeas and apricots and bring to a simmer.
  6. 6
    Cover and cook gently for 20 minutes until the chicken is cooked and the carrots are tender, then uncover briefly if the sauce needs thickening.
  7. 7
    Prepare the wholewheat couscous according to the packet instructions and fluff with half the lemon juice and chopped coriander.
  8. 8
    Brighten the tagine with the remaining lemon juice and serve it over the couscous.
