Chicken Tikka Flatbread Bowls

With Spiced Chickpeas, Crunchy Salad & Mint Yoghurt

Ingredients

  • 300 g Chicken Breast
  • 240 g Chickpeas
  • 1 each Wholemeal Pitta
  • 150 g Greek Yoghurt
  • 1 each Cucumber
  • 160 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 0.5 each Red Onion
  • 10 g Fresh Mint
  • 1 each Lime
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 2 tsp Mild Curry Powder
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 2 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

Method

  1. 1
    Heat the oven to 200°C fan and line a baking tray.
  2. 2
    Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and toss with half the yoghurt, half the Easy Garlic, the curry powder and half the lime juice.
  3. 3
    Drain the chickpeas, pat dry and toss with the smoked paprika, cumin and one teaspoon of rapeseed oil.
  4. 4
    Spread the chicken and chickpeas on the tray and roast for 16 to 20 minutes, turning once, until the chicken is cooked and the chickpeas are lightly crisp.
  5. 5
    Dice the cucumber and tomatoes, finely slice the red onion and toss together with a squeeze of lime.
  6. 6
    Chop the mint and stir it into the remaining yoghurt with the remaining Easy Garlic and lime juice.
  7. 7
    Toast the wholemeal pitta, cut it into wedges and divide it between two bowls with the salad.
  8. 8
    Add the tikka chicken and chickpeas, then spoon over the mint yoghurt and serve.
