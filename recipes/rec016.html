Chicken Shawarma Bowls

With Wholewheat Couscous, Spinach & Garlic Yoghurt

Ingredients

  • 300 g Chicken Breast
  • 150 g Wholewheat Couscous
  • 100 g Baby Spinach
  • 1 each Cucumber
  • 120 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 60 g Greek Yoghurt
  • 1.5 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Ground Coriander
  • 0.25 tsp Sumac
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Method

  1. 1
    Mix the chicken with extra virgin olive oil, cumin, smoked paprika, ground coriander, sumac and one teaspoon of Easy Garlic.
  2. 2
    Cook the chicken in a hot pan until browned and cooked through.
  3. 3
    Prepare the wholewheat couscous according to the packet instructions.
  4. 4
    Chop the baby spinach, cucumber, cherry tomatoes and red onion.
  5. 5
    Stir lemon juice and the remaining Easy Garlic into the Greek yoghurt.
  6. 6
    Fluff the couscous and divide it between bowls.
  7. 7
    Add the salad and top with the chicken.
  8. 8
    Spoon over the garlic yoghurt and serve.
