Chicken & Prawn Jambalaya

With Peppers, Celery & a Touch of Chorizo

Ingredients

  • For the jambalaya base

  • 400 g Chicken Breast
  • 50 g Chorizo
  • 240 g Brown Rice
  • 400 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 600 ml Low-Salt Chicken Stock
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 1 each Red Pepper
  • 1 each Green Pepper
  • 2 stick Celery
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 2 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 0.25 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

  • Add near the end

  • 200 g Prawns
  • 150 g Frozen Peas

Method

  1. 1
    Dice the chicken, chorizo, onion, peppers and celery into small, even pieces.
  2. 2
    Heat the rapeseed oil in a large lidded pan and cook the chorizo and chicken for five minutes until lightly coloured.
  3. 3
    Add the onion, peppers and celery and cook for five minutes until beginning to soften.
  4. 4
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, smoked paprika, thyme and cayenne and cook for 30 seconds.
  5. 5
    Add the brown rice, chopped tomatoes and low-salt stock, stir once and bring to a gentle simmer.
  6. 6
    Cover and cook for 20 to 25 minutes, or according to the rice packet, stirring once near the end and adding a splash of water if needed.
  7. 7
    Stir in the prawns and peas, cover again and cook for four to five minutes until the prawns are pink and the rice is tender.
  8. 8
    Rest off the heat for five minutes, fluff the rice and serve in warm bowls.
