Chicken & Prawn Jambalaya

With Peppers, Prawns & Smoky Chorizo

Ingredients

  • For the jambalaya base

  • 400 g Chicken Breast, diced.
  • 100 g Chorizo, finely diced.
  • 500 g Microwave Wholegrain Rice, unheated.
  • 400 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 2 cube Chicken Stock, dissolved in 150 ml boiling water.
  • 1 each White Onion, diced.
  • 1 each Red Pepper, diced.
  • 1 each Green Pepper, diced.
  • 1 stick Celery, diced.
  • 6 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 3 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 2 tsp Dried Parsley
  • 1 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 0.25 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

  • Add near the end

  • 200 g Prawns
  • 150 g Frozen Peas

  • Optional to finish

  • 10 g Fresh Parsley, chopped, optional.

Method

  1. 1
    Dice the chicken, chorizo, onion, peppers and celery into small, even pieces, and loosen the unheated wholegrain rice inside its pouches.
  2. 2
    Heat the rapeseed oil in a large, wide pan and cook the chorizo and chicken for five minutes until lightly coloured.
  3. 3
    Add the onion, peppers and celery and cook for five minutes until beginning to soften.
  4. 4
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, smoked paprika, dried parsley, thyme and cayenne and cook for 30 seconds.
  5. 5
    Dissolve the two chicken stock cubes in 150 ml boiling water, then add the stock with the chopped tomatoes. Simmer briskly for six to eight minutes with the lid off so the sauce reduces; if it still looks wet, raise the heat slightly and keep the lid off.
  6. 6
    Stir in the prawns and peas and cook uncovered for four to five minutes, until the prawns are pink and opaque and the chicken is cooked through with no pink remaining.
  7. 7
    Fold the unheated microwave wholegrain rice straight from the pouches into the pan and cook uncovered for three to four minutes, stirring regularly, until the rice is piping hot and has absorbed the sauce.
  8. 8
    Take the pan off the heat, rest for two minutes, then scatter over the chopped fresh parsley if using and serve in warm bowls.
