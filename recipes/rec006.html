Turkey & Bean Chilli

With Rice & Fresh Coriander

Ingredients

  • 350 g Turkey Mince
  • 200 g Red Kidney Beans
  • 400 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 each Red Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 200 g Basmati Rice
  • 5 g Fresh Coriander

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the basmati rice and keep it covered so it stays fluffy.
  2. 2
    Heat the olive oil in a pan and soften the onion and red pepper.
  3. 3
    Add the Easy Garlic and turkey mince, breaking the meat up as it browns.
  4. 4
    Stir in smoked paprika, cumin and chilli flakes.
  5. 5
    Pour in the chopped tomatoes and simmer for a few minutes.
  6. 6
    Add the red kidney beans and cook until the chilli thickens.
  7. 7
    Season to taste and stir through most of the fresh coriander.
  8. 8
    Serve the chilli over the rice with the remaining coriander on top.
