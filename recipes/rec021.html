Smoky Chicken, Butter Bean & Aubergine Stew

With Chorizo, Red Pepper & Rosemary

Ingredients

  • 500 g Chicken Breast
  • 400 g Butter Beans
  • 1 each Aubergine
  • 40 g Chorizo
  • 400 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 each Red Pepper
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 5 g Fresh Rosemary
  • 0.5 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces and finely dice the chorizo.
  2. 2
    Heat the extra virgin olive oil in a large casserole pan and cook the chorizo briefly until it releases its smoky oils.
  3. 3
    Add the chicken and brown it lightly on all sides.
  4. 4
    Add the fresh onion, red pepper and diced aubergine, then cook for 6–8 minutes until softened and lightly coloured.
  5. 5
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, smoked paprika, chopped rosemary and black pepper for one minute.
  6. 6
    Add the chopped tomatoes and drained butter beans, then bring to a gentle simmer.
  7. 7
    Cook uncovered until the sauce has thickened and the chicken is cooked through with no pink remaining.
  8. 8
    Stir through the red wine vinegar, check the seasoning without automatically adding salt, then serve in warm bowls.
