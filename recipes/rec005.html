Mediterranean Chicken Wrap

With Hummus, Feta & Baby Spinach

Ingredients

  • 300 g Chicken Breast
  • 2 each Wholemeal Wrap
  • 70 g Hummus
  • 35 g Feta
  • 100 g Baby Spinach
  • 1 each Cucumber
  • 120 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika

Method

  1. 1
    Season the chicken with black pepper, oregano and smoked paprika, then cook until golden and cooked through.
  2. 2
    Warm the wraps so they are easier to fold.
  3. 3
    Mix the baby spinach with cucumber, cherry tomatoes and red onion.
  4. 4
    Spread each wrap with hummus.
  5. 5
    Add the chicken on top of the hummus layer.
  6. 6
    Pile in the salad and crumble over the feta.
  7. 7
    Finish with lemon juice; the hummus will keep the wrap moist without extra oil.
  8. 8
    Roll up tightly and serve.
