Thai Green Basa Curry

With Peppers, Green Beans, Spinach & Brown Rice

Ingredients

  • For the green curry

  • 2 fillet Basa Fillet, cut into large chunks.
  • 200 ml Light Coconut Milk
  • 2 tbsp Thai Green Curry Paste
  • 1 each Red Pepper, sliced.
  • 160 g Green Beans, trimmed.
  • 100 g Baby Spinach
  • 2 each Spring Onions, sliced, whites and greens separated.
  • 1 tsp Easy Ginger
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 each Lime, juiced.
  • 1 tsp Fish Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

  • For the rice

  • 120 g Brown Rice

  • To finish

  • 1 each Spring Onions, finely sliced.
  • 15 g Fresh Coriander, chopped.

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the brown rice according to the packet instructions, then drain and keep warm.
  2. 2
    Cut the basa into large chunks, slice the pepper and spring onions and trim the green beans.
  3. 3
    Heat the rapeseed oil in a deep non-stick frying pan and cook the pepper and beans for four minutes.
  4. 4
    Add the Thai green curry paste, Easy Ginger, Easy Garlic and the white parts of the spring onions and stir for one minute.
  5. 5
    Pour in the light coconut milk and 150ml water, bring to a gentle simmer and cook for five minutes.
  6. 6
    Nestle in the basa and simmer gently for five to six minutes without vigorous stirring, until the fish is opaque and flakes easily.
  7. 7
    Fold in the spinach, lime juice and fish sauce and cook just until the spinach has wilted.
  8. 8
    Serve with the brown rice and finish with coriander and the green spring onion tops.
