Smoky Beef & Black Bean Stuffed Peppers

With Quinoa, Tomatoes & Lime Yoghurt

Ingredients

  • 400 g Lean Beef Mince (5%)
  • 2 each Red Pepper
  • 2 each Yellow Pepper
  • 240 g Black Beans
  • 120 g Quinoa
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 400 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 20 g Tomato Purée
  • 2 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 120 g Greek Yoghurt
  • 1 each Lime
  • 10 g Fresh Coriander

Method

  1. 1
    Heat the oven to 210°C/190°C fan and line a roasting tray.
  2. 2
    Halve the peppers lengthways through the stalks, remove the seeds and roast cut-side up for 12 minutes.
  3. 3
    Cook the quinoa according to the packet instructions, then drain well.
  4. 4
    Heat the olive oil in a non-stick pan and cook the chopped onion for five minutes, then add the beef and brown it thoroughly, breaking it into small pieces.
  5. 5
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, smoked paprika, cumin and tomato purée, then add the chopped tomatoes and simmer for eight minutes.
  6. 6
    Fold the drained black beans and cooked quinoa into the beef mixture and spoon generously into the pepper halves.
  7. 7
    Return the peppers to the oven for 12 to 15 minutes until tender and the filling is piping hot.
  8. 8
    Mix the Greek yoghurt with lime juice, then spoon it over the peppers and finish with chopped coriander.
