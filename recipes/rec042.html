Teriyaki Basa with Sesame Greens

With Brown Rice, Mange Tout & Sugar Snap Peas

Ingredients

  • 2 fillet Basa Fillet
  • 120 g Brown Rice
  • 120 g Mange Tout
  • 120 g Sugar Snap Peas
  • 3 each Spring Onions
  • 2 tbsp Low-Salt Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tbsp Rice Vinegar
  • 1 tsp Easy Ginger
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 2 tsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Seeds
  • 1 tsp Cornflour

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the brown rice according to the packet instructions, then drain and keep warm.
  2. 2
    Whisk the low-salt soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, Easy Ginger, Easy Garlic, cornflour and three tablespoons of water into a teriyaki sauce.
  3. 3
    Pat the basa dry and brush each fillet with a spoonful of the sauce.
  4. 4
    Heat one teaspoon of sesame oil in a non-stick frying pan and cook the basa for three to four minutes per side until golden and opaque.
  5. 5
    Transfer the fish to a warm plate, taking care to keep the fillets intact.
  6. 6
    Add the remaining sesame oil, mange tout and sugar snap peas to the pan and stir-fry for three minutes until bright and crisp-tender.
  7. 7
    Pour in the remaining sauce and simmer briefly until glossy, then return the fish to coat lightly.
  8. 8
    Serve with the brown rice and sesame greens and finish with sesame seeds and sliced spring onions.
