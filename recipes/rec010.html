Pesto Basa Pasta

With Cherry Tomatoes & Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 2 fillet Basa Fillet
  • 180 g Linguine
  • 100 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 25 g Parmesan
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 35 g Green Pesto
  • 80 g Baby Spinach
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 5 g Fresh Basil
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the linguine until al dente, reserving a little pasta water before draining.
  2. 2
    Heat the extra virgin olive oil in a pan and soften the onion and Easy Garlic.
  3. 3
    Add the basa fillets and cook gently until they start to flake.
  4. 4
    Stir in the green pesto and cherry tomatoes.
  5. 5
    Add the baby spinach and let it wilt into the sauce.
  6. 6
    Drain the pasta and toss it through the fish and pesto mixture.
  7. 7
    Finish with lemon juice, chopped basil and grated parmesan.
  8. 8
    Serve immediately while the sauce is glossy and warm.
