Chicken Shakshuka

With Spinach, Feta & Wholemeal Pitta

Ingredients

  • 200 g Chicken Breast
  • 2 each Egg
  • 400 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 1 each Red Pepper
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 50 g Feta
  • 80 g Baby Spinach
  • 0.5 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 2 each Wholemeal Pitta
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the chicken in a large pan until lightly browned and almost cooked through.
  2. 2
    Add extra virgin olive oil, onion and red pepper and soften them for a few minutes.
  3. 3
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, smoked paprika, cumin and chilli flakes.
  4. 4
    Pour in the chopped tomatoes and simmer until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. 5
    Stir in the baby spinach until wilted, then make two small wells in the sauce and crack in the eggs.
  6. 6
    Cover the pan and cook until the egg whites are set but the yolks are still soft.
  7. 7
    Scatter the feta over the top and warm the pittas separately.
  8. 8
    Serve the shakshuka with the pittas for scooping.
