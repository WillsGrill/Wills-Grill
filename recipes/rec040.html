Chicken & Refried Black Bean Tacos

With Zesty Apple Slaw & Chipotle Yoghurt

Ingredients

  • For the chicken

  • 300 g Chicken Breast
  • 0.5 each Red Onion
  • 1 tsp Chipotle Paste
  • 0.5 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tbsp Low-Salt Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

  • For the refried beans

  • 240 g Black Beans
  • 1 tsp Chipotle Paste
  • 0.5 tsp Easy Garlic

  • For the apple slaw

  • 1 each Apple
  • 100 g Red Cabbage
  • 0.5 each Red Onion
  • 0.5 each Lime
  • 1 tbsp Red Wine Vinegar

  • For the chipotle yoghurt

  • 0.5 each Lime
  • 100 g Greek Yoghurt

  • To serve

  • 6 each Small Wholemeal Tortillas

Method

  1. 1
    Finely shred the red cabbage, cut the apple into matchsticks and thinly slice half the red onion.
  2. 2
    Toss the cabbage, apple and sliced onion with the red wine vinegar and half the lime juice and set aside.
  3. 3
    Mash the drained black beans with half the Easy Garlic, half the chipotle paste and four tablespoons of water.
  4. 4
    Warm the bean mixture in a small pan for five minutes, stirring until creamy but still textured.
  5. 5
    Slice the chicken thinly, then cook it with the remaining chopped onion in the rapeseed oil until golden and cooked through.
  6. 6
    Add the smoked paprika, low-salt soy sauce and remaining chipotle and Easy Garlic and toss for two minutes.
  7. 7
    Mix the Greek yoghurt with the remaining lime juice and warm the tortillas in a dry pan.
  8. 8
    Spread each tortilla with refried beans, add the chicken and apple slaw and finish with chipotle-lime yoghurt.
