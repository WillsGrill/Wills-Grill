Halloumi & Roasted Vegetable Couscous

With Lemon & Fresh Herbs

Ingredients

  • 140 g Halloumi
  • 150 g Wholewheat Couscous
  • 150 g Chickpeas
  • 1 each Courgette
  • 1 each Aubergine
  • 2 each Red Pepper
  • 120 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 5 g Fresh Parsley
  • 5 g Fresh Mint
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the wholewheat couscous, then fluff it with a fork and keep it warm.
  2. 2
    Chop the vegetables into bite-sized pieces and toss with extra virgin olive oil, smoked paprika and black pepper.
  3. 3
    Roast the vegetables until soft and slightly browned.
  4. 4
    Slice the halloumi and fry it until golden on both sides.
  5. 5
    Drain the chickpeas and mix them into the couscous with the roasted vegetables.
  6. 6
    Stir through lemon juice and chopped parsley and mint.
  7. 7
    Top with the halloumi slices.
  8. 8
    Serve warm with an extra squeeze of lemon.
