Pork Souvlaki with Lemon Potatoes

With Greek Salad & Herby Yoghurt

Ingredients

  • For the pork souvlaki

  • 350 g Pork Tenderloin, cut into chunks.
  • 0.5 each Lemon, juiced.
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 0.5 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 0.5 tsp Black Pepper

  • For the lemon potatoes

  • 400 g New Potatoes, halved.
  • 0.5 each Lemon, juiced.
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil

  • For the Greek salad

  • 160 g Cherry Tomatoes, halved.
  • 1 each Cucumber, diced.
  • 0.5 each Red Onion, finely sliced.

  • For the herby yoghurt

  • 120 g Greek Yoghurt
  • 10 g Fresh Parsley, chopped.

Method

  1. 1
    Heat the oven to 200°C fan, halve the new potatoes and place them on a large baking tray.
  2. 2
    Toss the potatoes with one tablespoon of olive oil, half the oregano, half the Easy Garlic, half the lemon juice and black pepper, then roast for 25 to 30 minutes.
  3. 3
    Cut the pork tenderloin into chunks and toss with the remaining oregano, smoked paprika, Easy Garlic, lemon juice and olive oil.
  4. 4
    Thread the pork onto skewers, or leave as loose chunks if preferred.
  5. 5
    Cook the pork in a hot griddle or non-stick frying pan for eight to 10 minutes, turning regularly, until browned and cooked through.
  6. 6
    Halve the tomatoes, dice the cucumber and finely slice the red onion, then toss them together.
  7. 7
    Chop the parsley and mix it with the Greek yoghurt and a spoonful of water.
  8. 8
    Serve the pork with the lemon potatoes, Greek salad and herby yoghurt.
