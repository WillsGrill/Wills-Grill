Sticky Korean Chicken Noodle Salad

With Crunchy Slaw, Cucumber & Sesame-Lime Dressing

Ingredients

  • For the sticky chicken

  • 300 g Chicken Breast
  • 1 tsp Sesame Oil

  • For the dressing

  • 1 tbsp Gochujang Paste
  • 1 tbsp Low-Salt Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tbsp Rice Vinegar
  • 1 each Lime
  • 1 tsp Easy Ginger
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic

  • For the noodle salad

  • 200 g Wholemeal Udon Noodles
  • 200 g Rainbow Slaw Mix
  • 1 each Cucumber
  • 3 each Spring Onions

  • To finish

  • 1 tbsp Sesame Seeds

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the wholemeal udon according to the packet instructions, rinse under cold water and drain well.
  2. 2
    Whisk the gochujang, low-salt soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, lime juice, Easy Ginger, Easy Garlic and two tablespoons of water.
  3. 3
    Slice the chicken thinly and coat it with one third of the dressing.
  4. 4
    Heat the sesame oil in a large non-stick frying pan and cook the chicken for six to eight minutes until browned and cooked through.
  5. 5
    Pour another third of the dressing into the pan and toss for one minute until the chicken is glossy and sticky.
  6. 6
    Cut the cucumber into ribbons and finely slice the spring onions.
  7. 7
    Toss the noodles with the slaw, cucumber, most of the spring onions and the remaining dressing.
  8. 8
    Divide between bowls, top with the sticky chicken and finish with sesame seeds and the remaining spring onions.
