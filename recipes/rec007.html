Tuna & Cannellini Bean Salad

With Cherry Tomatoes, Mixed Leaves & Honey-Lemon Dressing

Ingredients

  • For the salad

  • 290 g Tinned Tuna in Spring Water, drained.
  • 200 g Cannellini Beans, drained and rinsed.
  • 120 g Cherry Tomatoes, halved.
  • 80 g Mixed Salad Leaves
  • 1 each Cucumber, sliced.
  • 1 each Apple, cored and diced.
  • 5 g Fresh Parsley, chopped.

  • For the dressing

  • 2 tbsp Bottled Lemon Juice
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Capers, drained.
  • 0.333 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Drain the tinned tuna thoroughly and break it into large flakes with a fork.
  2. 2
    Drain and rinse the cannellini beans, then leave them to dry briefly.
  3. 3
    Whisk together the bottled lemon juice, honey, olive oil, capers and black pepper.
  4. 4
    Slice the cherry tomatoes and cucumber, then core and dice the apple.
  5. 5
    Toss the mixed leaves with the beans, tomatoes, cucumber and diced apple in a large bowl.
  6. 6
    Gently fold the tuna flakes through the salad without breaking them up too much.
  7. 7
    Scatter over the chopped parsley and drizzle with the lemon dressing.
  8. 8
    Serve straight away while the leaves and apple are crisp.
