Tuna & Cannellini Bean Salad

With Cherry Tomatoes, Rocket & Lemon Dressing

Ingredients

  • 2 steak Tuna Steak
  • 200 g Cannellini Beans
  • 120 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 80 g Rocket
  • 1 each Cucumber
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Capers
  • 5 g Fresh Parsley
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Season the tuna steaks and sear them quickly in a hot pan.
  2. 2
    Drain and rinse the cannellini beans.
  3. 3
    Whisk together lemon juice, lemon zest, olive oil, capers and black pepper.
  4. 4
    Slice the cherry tomatoes, cucumber and red onion.
  5. 5
    Toss the rocket with the beans and vegetables in a large bowl.
  6. 6
    Slice the tuna and lay it over the salad.
  7. 7
    Scatter over the chopped parsley and drizzle with the lemon dressing.
  8. 8
    Serve while the tuna is still warm.
