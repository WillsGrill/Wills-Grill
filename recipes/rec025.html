Healthy Chicken, Chorizo & Seafood Paella

With Prawns, Mussels & Peas

Ingredients

  • 400 g Chicken Breast
  • 50 g Chorizo
  • 200 g Prawns
  • 400 g Mussels
  • 300 g Paella Rice
  • 750 ml Low-Salt Chicken Stock
  • 200 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 each Red Pepper
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 150 g Frozen Peas
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 0.25 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 10 g Fresh Parsley
  • 0.5 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Warm the low-salt chicken stock and stir in the turmeric while you prepare the other ingredients.
  2. 2
    Heat the extra virgin olive oil in a wide paella pan, cook the finely diced chorizo briefly, then add and lightly brown the chicken.
  3. 3
    Add the fresh onion and red pepper and cook until softened.
  4. 4
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, smoked paprika, black pepper and paella rice until every grain is coated.
  5. 5
    Add the chopped tomatoes and seasoned stock, spread the rice evenly and simmer gently without stirring until most of the liquid is absorbed.
  6. 6
    Nestle the cleaned mussels into the rice, cover loosely and cook until they begin to open.
  7. 7
    Add the prawns and frozen peas and cook until the prawns are opaque, the mussels are open and the chicken is cooked through; discard any mussels that remain closed.
  8. 8
    Rest for five minutes, then finish with lemon juice, lemon wedges and chopped parsley.
