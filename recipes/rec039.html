Pork & Refried Black Bean Tacos

With Zesty Apple Slaw & Chipotle Yoghurt

Ingredients

  • 250 g Pork Mince
  • 240 g Black Beans
  • 6 each Small Wholemeal Tortillas
  • 1 each Apple
  • 100 g Red Cabbage
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 2 tsp Chipotle Paste
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 each Lime
  • 100 g Greek Yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp Red Wine Vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Low-Salt Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

Method

  1. 1
    Finely shred the red cabbage, cut the apple into matchsticks and thinly slice half the red onion.
  2. 2
    Toss the cabbage, apple and sliced onion with the red wine vinegar and half the lime juice and set aside.
  3. 3
    Mash the drained black beans with half the Easy Garlic, half the chipotle paste and four tablespoons of water.
  4. 4
    Warm the bean mixture in a small pan for five minutes, stirring until creamy but still textured.
  5. 5
    Heat the rapeseed oil in a frying pan and cook the remaining chopped onion and pork mince until browned and completely cooked.
  6. 6
    Add the smoked paprika, low-salt soy sauce and remaining chipotle and Easy Garlic and cook for two minutes.
  7. 7
    Mix the Greek yoghurt with the remaining lime juice and warm the tortillas in a dry pan.
  8. 8
    Spread each tortilla with refried beans, add the pork and apple slaw and finish with chipotle-lime yoghurt.
