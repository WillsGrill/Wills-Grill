Chicken, Spinach & Chickpea Curry

With Wholegrain Brown Rice & Fresh Coriander

Ingredients

  • 375 g Chicken Breast
  • 400 g Chickpeas
  • 120 g Baby Spinach
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 400 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 500 g Brown Rice
  • 1 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Ground Coriander
  • 1 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 1 tsp Ground Ginger
  • 5 g Fresh Coriander
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the wholegrain brown rice according to the packet instructions and keep it covered while you make the curry.
  2. 2
    Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces, then heat the extra virgin olive oil in a large pan.
  3. 3
    Brown the chicken on all sides, add the onion and cook until softened.
  4. 4
    Add the Easy Garlic, turmeric, cumin, ground coriander, ground ginger and chilli flakes, then stir for one minute.
  5. 5
    Stir in the chickpeas and chopped tomatoes and bring the sauce to a gentle simmer.
  6. 6
    Cook until the sauce thickens and the chicken is cooked through with no pink remaining.
  7. 7
    Add the baby spinach, let it wilt, then season and stir through most of the fresh coriander.
  8. 8
    Serve the curry over the rice with the remaining coriander on top.
