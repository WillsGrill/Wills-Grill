Angel's Wings Chicken Grain Bowls

With Mediterranean Vegetables & Garlic Yoghurt

Ingredients

  • 300 g Chicken Breast
  • 1 tbsp Angel's Wings Rub
  • 120 g Bulgur Wheat
  • 1 each Courgette
  • 0.5 each Aubergine
  • 1 each Red Pepper
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 150 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 150 g Greek Yoghurt
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 10 g Fresh Parsley

Method

  1. 1
    Heat the oven to 220°C/200°C fan and line a large roasting tray.
  2. 2
    Cut the courgette, aubergine, red pepper and red onion into bite-sized pieces, add the cherry tomatoes and toss everything with half the olive oil.
  3. 3
    Roast the vegetables for 20 to 25 minutes, turning once, until tender and browned at the edges.
  4. 4
    Coat the chicken breasts with the Angel's Wings rub and remaining olive oil, then grill or pan-cook for five to seven minutes per side until cooked through.
  5. 5
    Cook the bulgur wheat according to the packet instructions, drain well and fluff with half the lemon juice and chopped parsley.
  6. 6
    Mix the Greek yoghurt with the crushed garlic, remaining lemon juice and enough cold water to make a spoonable sauce.
  7. 7
    Rest the chicken for five minutes, then slice it across the grain.
  8. 8
    Divide the bulgur and roasted vegetables between bowls, add the chicken and spoon over the garlic yoghurt sauce.
