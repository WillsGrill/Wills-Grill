Sesame, Honey & Lime Basa

With Sweet Potato Wedges & Fresh Coriander

Ingredients

  • For the glazed basa

  • 2 fillet Basa Fillet
  • 2 tsp Cornflour
  • 1 tsp Easy Ginger
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 each Lime, zested and juiced.
  • 0.5 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tbsp Low-Salt Soy Sauce
  • 0.5 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

  • For the sweet potato wedges

  • 450 g Sweet Potatoes, cut into slim wedges.
  • 0.5 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

  • To finish

  • 15 g Fresh Coriander, chopped.
  • 2 each Spring Onions, sliced.
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Seeds

Method

  1. 1
    Heat the oven to 220°C/200°C fan and line a large tray with baking paper.
  2. 2
    Cut the sweet potatoes into slim wedges, toss with half the rapeseed oil and black pepper and roast for 25 to 30 minutes, turning once.
  3. 3
    Mix the honey, lime zest and juice, low-salt soy sauce, Easy Ginger and Easy Garlic.
  4. 4
    Pat the basa dry, dust lightly with cornflour and black pepper and brush with half the honey-lime mixture.
  5. 5
    Heat the remaining oil in a non-stick frying pan and cook the basa for three to four minutes per side until golden and opaque.
  6. 6
    Add the remaining honey-lime mixture to the pan for the final minute and turn the fish carefully until lightly glazed.
  7. 7
    Scatter the sesame seeds over the fish and check that it flakes easily at its thickest point.
  8. 8
    Serve with the sweet potato wedges and finish with chopped coriander and sliced spring onion.
