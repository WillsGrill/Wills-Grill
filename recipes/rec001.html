Greek Chicken Salad

With Feta, Olives & Lemon Dressing

Ingredients

  • 300 g Chicken Breast
  • 120 g Baby Spinach
  • 120 g Cannellini Beans
  • 160 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 each Cucumber
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 60 g Feta
  • 40 g Black Olives
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano

Method

  1. 1
    Slice the chicken breast into strips and season with black pepper and dried oregano.
  2. 2
    Heat one tablespoon of olive oil in a pan and cook the chicken until golden and cooked through.
  3. 3
    Whisk the lemon juice and zest with the remaining olive oil to make a simple dressing.
  4. 4
    Drain the cannellini beans and arrange them with the baby spinach, cherry tomatoes, cucumber and red onion in bowls.
  5. 5
    Add the warm chicken on top of the salad.
  6. 6
    Scatter over the feta and black olives.
  7. 7
    Drizzle with the lemon dressing and toss lightly.
  8. 8
    Serve straight away while the chicken is still warm.
