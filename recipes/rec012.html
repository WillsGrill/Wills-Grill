Herb Crusted Cod

With Garlic Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients

  • For the herb-crusted cod

  • 2 fillet Cod Fillet
  • 60 g Panko Breadcrumbs
  • 5 g Fresh Parsley
  • 1 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 1 tsp Dijon Mustard

  • For the roasted vegetables

  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 each Courgette
  • 1 each Aubergine
  • 1 each Red Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 0.25 tsp Sea Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Preheat the oven and chop the courgette, aubergine and red pepper into even pieces.
  2. 2
    Toss the vegetables with extra virgin olive oil, Easy Garlic, salt and pepper.
  3. 3
    Roast the vegetables until tender and lightly browned.
  4. 4
    Mix the panko with chopped parsley, dried thyme and lemon zest.
  5. 5
    Brush the cod with Dijon mustard and press the herb crumb onto the top.
  6. 6
    Bake the cod until it flakes easily and the topping is golden.
  7. 7
    Squeeze lemon over the fish and vegetables.
  8. 8
    Serve the cod with the garlic roasted vegetables.
