Korean-Style Gochujang Turkey

With Spinach Rice, Cucumber & Egg

Ingredients

  • 300 g Turkey Mince
  • 120 g Brown Rice
  • 120 g Baby Spinach
  • 0.5 each Cucumber
  • 2 each Egg
  • 1.5 tbsp Gochujang Paste
  • 1 tbsp Low-Salt Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tbsp Rice Vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Seeds
  • 2 tsp Sesame Oil
  • 2 each Spring Onions

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the brown rice according to the packet instructions, adding the spinach for the final two minutes so it wilts into the rice.
  2. 2
    Whisk the gochujang, low-salt soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar and two tablespoons of water into a smooth sauce.
  3. 3
    Cut the cucumber into thin half-moons and toss with a teaspoon of the sauce, then set aside.
  4. 4
    Heat one teaspoon of sesame oil in a non-stick frying pan and brown the turkey mince for six to eight minutes, breaking it into small pieces.
  5. 5
    Pour in the remaining gochujang sauce and cook for two minutes until the turkey is glossy and fully cooked.
  6. 6
    Fry the eggs in the remaining sesame oil until the whites are set and the yolks are cooked to your liking.
  7. 7
    Divide the spinach rice between bowls and top with the gochujang turkey, cucumber and eggs.
  8. 8
    Finish with sesame seeds and finely sliced spring onions.
