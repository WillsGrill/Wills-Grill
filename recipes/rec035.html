Marmite & Peanut Butter Pork Udon

With Spinach, Spring Onion & Roasted Peanuts

Ingredients

  • 250 g Pork Mince
  • 300 g Wholemeal Udon Noodles
  • 120 g Baby Spinach
  • 3 each Spring Onions
  • 35 g Natural Peanut Butter
  • 20 g Roasted Peanuts
  • 2 tsp Marmite
  • 1 tbsp Red Chilli Jam
  • 1 tbsp Low-Salt Soy Sauce
  • 1 tsp Easy Ginger
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

Method

  1. 1
    Whisk the peanut butter, Marmite, chilli jam, low-salt soy sauce, Easy Ginger, Easy Garlic and five tablespoons of hot water until smooth.
  2. 2
    Cook the wholemeal udon according to the packet instructions, then drain and reserve a little cooking water.
  3. 3
    Heat the rapeseed oil in a large non-stick frying pan or wok.
  4. 4
    Add the lean pork mince and cook over a high heat for six to seven minutes, breaking it up, until browned and cooked through.
  5. 5
    Add the sliced spring onions, reserving a few green slices for serving, and cook for one minute.
  6. 6
    Add the spinach and toss until just wilted.
  7. 7
    Add the noodles and sauce and toss for two minutes, loosening with reserved cooking water until glossy but not heavy.
  8. 8
    Divide between bowls and finish with the reserved spring onion and roughly chopped roasted peanuts.
