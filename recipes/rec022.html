Turkey & Red Lentil Bolognese

With Wholemeal Penne, Spinach & Fresh Basil

Ingredients

  • 400 g Turkey Mince
  • 150 g Red Lentils
  • 400 g Passata
  • 300 g Wholemeal Penne
  • 160 g Baby Spinach
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 5 g Fresh Basil
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 0.5 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Rinse the red lentils under cold water and leave them to drain.
  2. 2
    Heat the extra virgin olive oil in a large pan and soften the fresh onion.
  3. 3
    Add the turkey mince and cook until lightly browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  4. 4
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, dried oregano and black pepper for one minute.
  5. 5
    Add the passata and red lentils with enough water to keep the sauce loose, then simmer until the lentils are tender.
  6. 6
    Cook the wholemeal penne until al dente while the sauce simmers, reserving a little pasta water.
  7. 7
    Wilt the baby spinach into the bolognese and loosen with pasta water if needed.
  8. 8
    Toss with the penne, stir through the fresh basil and serve.
