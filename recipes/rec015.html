Lemon Garlic Basa & Prawn Rice

With Cherry Tomatoes, Spinach & Fresh Parsley

Ingredients

  • 2 fillet Basa Fillet
  • 250 g Prawns
  • 250 g Brown Rice
  • 160 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 5 g Fresh Parsley
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 80 g Baby Spinach
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 0.25 tsp Sea Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 0.5 tsp Chilli Flakes

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the wholegrain brown rice according to the packet instructions and keep it covered.
  2. 2
    Pat the basa dry, season with black pepper and cook it gently in a large frying pan until it begins to flake.
  3. 3
    Move the basa to the edge of the pan, add the extra virgin olive oil and soften the Easy Garlic with the chilli flakes.
  4. 4
    Add the cherry tomatoes and cook until they soften and begin to burst.
  5. 5
    Stir in the prawns and cook until pink, opaque and cooked through, keeping the basa in large flakes.
  6. 6
    Add the baby spinach and let it wilt into the tomato and prawn mixture.
  7. 7
    Fold in the cooked brown rice, lemon juice and chopped parsley, then season with the measured salt and black pepper.
  8. 8
    Serve immediately with an extra squeeze of lemon if desired.
