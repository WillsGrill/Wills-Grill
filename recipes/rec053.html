Cajun Salmon with Sweet Potato & Corn Salsa

With Tomatoes, Lime & Coriander

Ingredients

  • 2 fillet Salmon Fillet
  • 400 g Sweet Potatoes
  • 160 g Sweetcorn
  • 160 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 0.5 each Red Onion
  • 1 each Lime
  • 15 g Fresh Coriander
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Sweet Paprika
  • 0.5 tsp Garlic Granules
  • 0.5 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 0.25 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Rapeseed Oil
  • 80 g Greek Yoghurt

Method

  1. 1
    Heat the oven to 200°C fan and line a large baking tray.
  2. 2
    Cut the sweet potato into 2cm cubes, toss with one teaspoon of rapeseed oil and half the smoked paprika, then roast for 15 minutes.
  3. 3
    Mix the remaining smoked paprika with the sweet paprika, garlic granules, thyme and cayenne.
  4. 4
    Rub the salmon with the remaining rapeseed oil and the spice mix, then place it on the tray beside the sweet potato.
  5. 5
    Roast everything for a further 10 to 12 minutes until the salmon flakes easily and the sweet potato is tender.
  6. 6
    Meanwhile, drain the sweetcorn, halve the tomatoes, finely dice the red onion and chop the coriander.
  7. 7
    Toss the salsa ingredients with the lime zest and juice, then stir a spoonful of lime juice into the Greek yoghurt.
  8. 8
    Serve the Cajun salmon with the roasted sweet potato, corn salsa and lime yoghurt.
