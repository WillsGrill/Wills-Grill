Herb & Lemon Mackerel with Spanish Grains

With Tenderstem Broccoli, Mange Tout & Lemon

Ingredients

  • 2 fillet Mackerel Fillet
  • 150 g Spanish Grains
  • 120 g Tenderstem Broccoli
  • 100 g Mange Tout
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 5 g Fresh Dill
  • 0.25 tsp Sea Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Heat the oven and cook the wholegrain Spanish grains according to the packet instructions.
  2. 2
    Mix the extra virgin olive oil with the chopped dill, Easy Garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, salt and black pepper.
  3. 3
    Place each mackerel fillet on its own sheet of foil and spoon over the herb and lemon blend.
  4. 4
    Seal each foil parcel loosely, place on a baking tray and bake until the mackerel flakes easily.
  5. 5
    Steam or quickly pan-cook the tenderstem broccoli and mange tout so they remain bright and crisp.
  6. 6
    Open the foil parcels carefully and spoon the warm herb and lemon juices back over the fish.
  7. 7
    Fluff the Spanish grains and check the seasoning of the vegetables.
  8. 8
    Serve each mackerel parcel with the Spanish grains and vegetables.
