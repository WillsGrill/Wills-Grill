Healthy Traditional Beef Chilli Con Carne

With Kidney Beans, Peppers & Brown Rice

Ingredients

  • 500 g Lean Beef Mince (5%)
  • 240 g Brown Rice
  • 240 g Red Kidney Beans
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 1 each Red Pepper
  • 150 g Carrots
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 800 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 30 g Tomato Purée
  • 1 cube Beef Stock
  • 2 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 2 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 0.5 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 0.25 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 120 g Greek Yoghurt
  • 10 g Fresh Coriander

Method

  1. 1
    Heat the olive oil in a large heavy-based pan and cook the finely chopped onion, red pepper and grated carrot for eight minutes until soft.
  2. 2
    Add the lean beef mince and cook over a medium-high heat until deeply browned, breaking it up with a wooden spoon.
  3. 3
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, cumin, smoked paprika, chilli flakes and cinnamon and cook for one minute until fragrant.
  4. 4
    Add the tomato purée, chopped tomatoes, crumbled beef stock cube and 250ml water and bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. 5
    Part-cover and cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has reduced and the vegetables are tender.
  6. 6
    Rinse and drain the kidney beans, stir them into the chilli and simmer uncovered for a further 10 minutes.
  7. 7
    Meanwhile, cook the brown rice according to the packet instructions and drain well.
  8. 8
    Serve the chilli over the rice with Greek yoghurt and chopped coriander.
