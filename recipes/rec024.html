BBQ Chicken and Halloumi Caprese Skewers

With Pesto Quinoa, Mozzarella & Fresh Basil

Ingredients

  • For the skewers

  • 250 g Chicken Breast, cut into bite-sized pieces.
  • 1 each Courgette, cut into bite-sized pieces.
  • 1 each Red Pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces.
  • 150 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 100 g Halloumi, cut into bite-sized pieces.
  • 20 g Green Pesto
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 0.25 tsp Black Pepper

  • For the pesto quinoa

  • 120 g Quinoa
  • 20 g Green Pesto
  • 1 each Lemon, zested and juiced.
  • 8 g Fresh Basil, torn.

  • To serve

  • 50 g Mozzarella, torn.
  • 2 g Fresh Basil, torn.

Method

  1. 1
    Light the BBQ for direct cooking and, if using wooden skewers, soak them in water while it heats.
  2. 2
    Cut the chicken, halloumi, courgette and red pepper into even bite-sized pieces.
  3. 3
    Thread the chicken, halloumi, vegetables and cherry tomatoes onto skewers, then brush with half the pesto mixed with the olive oil, Easy Garlic and black pepper.
  4. 4
    Cook the skewers on the BBQ, turning regularly, until the vegetables are charred and the chicken is cooked through.
  5. 5
    Cook the quinoa according to the packet instructions, then fluff it with a fork.
  6. 6
    Stir the remaining pesto, lemon juice, lemon zest and most of the fresh basil through the quinoa.
  7. 7
    Rest the skewers briefly, then serve them over the pesto quinoa.
  8. 8
    Tear over the mozzarella and finish with the remaining basil.
