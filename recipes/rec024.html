BBQ Chicken Caprese Skewers

With Pesto Quinoa, Mozzarella & Fresh Basil

Ingredients

  • 600 g Chicken Breast
  • 240 g Quinoa
  • 2 each Courgette
  • 2 each Red Pepper
  • 300 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 100 g Mozzarella
  • 40 g Green Pesto
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 10 g Fresh Basil
  • 0.5 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    If using wooden skewers, soak them in water while the BBQ heats for direct cooking.
  2. 2
    Cut the chicken, courgettes and red peppers into even bite-sized pieces.
  3. 3
    Thread the chicken, vegetables and cherry tomatoes onto skewers, then brush with half the pesto mixed with Easy Garlic and black pepper.
  4. 4
    Cook the skewers on the BBQ, turning regularly, until the vegetables are charred and the chicken is cooked through.
  5. 5
    Cook the quinoa according to the packet instructions, then fluff it with a fork.
  6. 6
    Stir the remaining pesto, lemon juice, lemon zest and most of the fresh basil through the quinoa.
  7. 7
    Rest the skewers briefly, then serve them over the pesto quinoa.
  8. 8
    Tear over the mozzarella and finish with the remaining basil.
