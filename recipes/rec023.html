BBQ Turkey Breast with Butter Bean Salad

With Roasted Peppers, Cherry Tomatoes & Parsley

Ingredients

  • 600 g Turkey Breast
  • 400 g Butter Beans
  • 200 g Roasted Red Peppers
  • 250 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 2 each Lemon
  • 2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 2 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 10 g Fresh Parsley
  • 0.5 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Coat the turkey breast with one tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil, Easy Garlic, smoked paprika, oregano and black pepper.
  2. 2
    Heat the BBQ for direct cooking and allow the turkey to marinate while it comes to temperature.
  3. 3
    Drain and rinse the butter beans, then slice the roasted red peppers, cherry tomatoes and red onion.
  4. 4
    Combine the beans and vegetables in a large serving bowl.
  5. 5
    Whisk the lemon juice and zest with the remaining olive oil and chopped parsley, then fold through the salad.
  6. 6
    Cook the turkey on the BBQ until browned and cooked through with no pink remaining.
  7. 7
    Rest the turkey for five minutes before slicing it across the grain.
  8. 8
    Serve the sliced turkey over the butter bean salad with any remaining lemon dressing.
