Thai Fishcakes

With Sweet Chilli Dip & Asian Slaw

Ingredients

  • 300 g Pollock Fillet
  • 1 each Egg
  • 60 g Panko Breadcrumbs
  • 2 each Spring Onions
  • 5 g Fresh Coriander
  • 1 each Lime
  • 1 tbsp Low-Salt Soy Sauce
  • 1 tsp Sesame Oil
  • 0.5 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 200 g Rainbow Slaw Mix
  • 80 g Greek Yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 0.25 tsp Sea Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Flake the pollock and mix it with the egg, panko, spring onions and coriander.
  2. 2
    Season the mixture with salt, pepper and a squeeze of lime.
  3. 3
    Shape into small fishcakes and chill briefly if the mixture feels soft.
  4. 4
    Mix the Greek yoghurt, honey, chilli flakes and a little lime juice for the dip.
  5. 5
    Toss the bagged rainbow slaw with sesame oil, low-salt soy sauce and lime.
  6. 6
    Place the fishcakes on a lined baking tray, brush lightly with extra virgin olive oil and bake until crisp, golden and cooked through.
  7. 7
    Serve the fishcakes with the sweet chilli dip and the slaw on the side.
  8. 8
    Finish with extra coriander if you want a fresher flavour.
