Healthy Chicken Gyros

With Homemade Tzatziki, Salad & Warm Pitta

Ingredients

  • For the chicken

  • 300 g Chicken Breast
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 0.5 tsp Black Pepper

  • For the gyros

  • 2 each Wholemeal Pitta
  • 120 g Tzatziki
  • 100 g Baby Spinach
  • 1 each Cucumber
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 120 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 50 g Feta
  • 1 each Lemon

Method

  1. 1
    Season the chicken with extra virgin olive oil, oregano, smoked paprika and black pepper.
  2. 2
    Cook the chicken in a hot pan until it is browned and cooked through.
  3. 3
    Mix the baby spinach, cucumber, cherry tomatoes and red onion in a bowl.
  4. 4
    Warm the pittas.
  5. 5
    Stir a little lemon juice into the tzatziki for a brighter flavour.
  6. 6
    Fill each pitta with the salad and chicken.
  7. 7
    Add the feta and spoon over the tzatziki.
  8. 8
    Serve immediately while the pittas are still warm.
