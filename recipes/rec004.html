BBQ Chicken Souvlaki

With Homemade Garlic Tzatziki, Charred Pepper, Greek Salad & Warm Pitta

Ingredients

  • For the chicken

  • 300 g Chicken Breast, cut into strips.
  • 0.5 each Lemon, zested and juiced.
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

  • For the Greek salad

  • 0.5 each Cucumber, diced.
  • 160 g Cherry Tomatoes, halved.
  • 1 each Red Onion, finely sliced.
  • 50 g Feta, crumbled.
  • 40 g Black Olives

  • For the charred pepper

  • 1 each Red Pepper, quartered and deseeded.

  • For the garlic tzatziki

  • 150 g Greek Yoghurt
  • 0.5 each Cucumber, coarsely grated.
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 0.5 each Lemon, juiced.
  • 5 g Fresh Dill, finely chopped.

  • To serve

  • 2 each Wholemeal Pitta

Method

  1. 1
    Squeeze the grated cucumber firmly to remove excess water, then mix it with the Greek yoghurt, 2 teaspoons Easy Garlic, dill and the juice of half a lemon; season with black pepper and chill while you cook.
  2. 2
    Coat the chicken strips with the olive oil, remaining Easy Garlic, oregano, black pepper and the zest and juice of half a lemon.
  3. 3
    Heat a large griddle pan or barbecue until very hot, add the pepper quarters and cook for 3 minutes to give them a head start.
  4. 4
    Add the chicken to the griddle alongside the pepper and cook everything for 6 to 8 minutes, turning regularly, until the pepper is softened and charred and the chicken has no pink remaining.
  5. 5
    Toss the diced cucumber, halved tomatoes, sliced red onion, feta and olives together, then slice the charred pepper into strips.
  6. 6
    Warm the wholemeal pittas in a dry pan or oven until soft and pliable.
  7. 7
    Split each pitta and fill with the Greek salad, charred pepper and hot chicken strips.
  8. 8
    Spoon over the garlic tzatziki and serve immediately with any remaining salad and tzatziki alongside.
