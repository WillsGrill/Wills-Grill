BBQ Chicken Souvlaki

With Tzatziki, Greek Salad & Warm Pitta

Ingredients

  • 300 g Chicken Breast
  • 2 each Wholemeal Pitta
  • 120 g Tzatziki
  • 1 each Cucumber
  • 160 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 50 g Feta
  • 40 g Black Olives
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Cut the chicken into strips and marinate with extra virgin olive oil, Easy Garlic, oregano, lemon juice and zest, and black pepper.
  2. 2
    Cook the chicken on a hot griddle, lined baking tray or air-fryer basket until nicely charred and cooked through.
  3. 3
    Prepare a Greek salad with cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, feta and olives.
  4. 4
    Warm the pittas in a dry pan or oven.
  5. 5
    Stir the tzatziki so it is smooth and ready to spoon over the chicken.
  6. 6
    Pile the salad into each pitta and top with the chicken strips.
  7. 7
    Add a generous spoonful of tzatziki and a little extra lemon if needed.
  8. 8
    Serve with the remaining salad on the side.
