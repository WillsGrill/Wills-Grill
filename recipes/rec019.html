Chicken Baked Feta Pasta

With Roasted Cherry Tomatoes & Fresh Basil

Ingredients

  • 250 g Chicken Breast
  • 140 g Feta
  • 180 g Wholemeal Penne
  • 350 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 5 g Fresh Basil
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 80 g Baby Spinach
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 0.5 tsp Chilli Flakes

Method

  1. 1
    Heat the oven and cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces.
  2. 2
    Place the cherry tomatoes, onion and Easy Garlic in a baking dish, drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and season with oregano, chilli flakes and black pepper.
  3. 3
    Nestle the block of feta into the centre, arrange the chicken around it and turn the chicken in the seasoned oil.
  4. 4
    Bake until the tomatoes are soft, the feta is melting and the chicken is cooked through with no pink remaining.
  5. 5
    Cook the wholemeal penne until al dente while the chicken and tomatoes roast, reserving a little pasta water.
  6. 6
    Mash the feta and tomatoes together, then stir in enough pasta water to make a silky sauce.
  7. 7
    Wilt the baby spinach into the sauce, then toss through the penne, cooked chicken and fresh basil.
  8. 8
    Check the seasoning and serve immediately.
