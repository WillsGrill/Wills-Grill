Chicken Pitta with Tzatziki

With Salad, Hummus, Tomatoes & Red Onion

Ingredients

  • For the chicken

  • 300 g Chicken Breast, cut into strips.
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika

  • For the pitta filling

  • 2 each Wholemeal Pitta
  • 60 g Hummus
  • 100 g Baby Spinach
  • 120 g Cherry Tomatoes, sliced.
  • 1 each Red Onion, finely sliced.
  • 30 g Feta, crumbled.

  • For the tzatziki

  • 80 g Tzatziki
  • 1 each Lemon, juiced.

Method

  1. 1
    Season the chicken with black pepper, oregano and smoked paprika, then cook it in a hot pan until browned and fully cooked.
  2. 2
    Warm the wholemeal pittas so they are soft and ready to fill.
  3. 3
    Slice the tomatoes and red onion and toss them with the baby spinach.
  4. 4
    Stir a little lemon juice into the tzatziki for extra freshness.
  5. 5
    Split the pittas and spread each one with hummus.
  6. 6
    Fill with salad, chicken, feta and red onion.
  7. 7
    Spoon in the tzatziki and finish with a squeeze of lemon.
  8. 8
    Serve immediately while the pittas are warm.
