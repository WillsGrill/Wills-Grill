Turkey Meatballs in Tomato Sauce

With Wholemeal Pasta & Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 350 g Turkey Mince
  • 1 each Egg
  • 40 g Breadcrumbs
  • 30 g Parmesan
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 400 g Passata
  • 180 g Wholemeal Penne
  • 120 g Baby Spinach
  • 5 g Fresh Basil
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 0.25 tsp Sea Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Mix the turkey mince with the egg, breadcrumbs, parmesan, salt and pepper.
  2. 2
    Shape the mixture into meatballs.
  3. 3
    Brown the meatballs in olive oil in a large pan.
  4. 4
    Add the fresh onion and Easy Garlic and soften them gently.
  5. 5
    Pour in the passata and simmer until the sauce thickens.
  6. 6
    Cook the wholemeal penne until al dente.
  7. 7
    Wilt the baby spinach into the sauce, then toss through the wholemeal penne, meatballs and basil.
  8. 8
    Serve with extra parmesan scattered on top.
