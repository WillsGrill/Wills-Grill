Turkey Meatballs in Tomato Sauce

With Wholemeal Pasta & Parmesan

Ingredients

  • For the meatballs

  • 250 g Turkey Mince
  • 1 each Egg
  • 30 g Breadcrumbs
  • 15 g Parmesan
  • 0.25 tsp Sea Salt
  • 0.75 tsp Black Pepper

  • For the tomato sauce

  • 0.5 each White Onion
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 0.5 tsp Marmite
  • 250 g Passata
  • 60 g Baby Spinach
  • 0.5 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil

  • For the pasta

  • 90 g Wholemeal Penne

  • To finish

  • 2.5 g Fresh Basil
  • 5 g Parmesan

Method

  1. 1
    Mix the turkey mince with the egg, breadcrumbs, 15g parmesan, salt and black pepper until just combined.
  2. 2
    With damp hands, shape the mixture into 12 evenly sized meatballs.
  3. 3
    Heat the extra virgin olive oil in a large frying pan over a medium-high heat and brown the meatballs all over for 5 minutes.
  4. 4
    Push the meatballs to one side, add the onion and soften for 4 minutes, then stir in the Easy Garlic for 1 minute.
  5. 5
    Pour in the passata, stir in the Marmite and simmer gently for 12 to 15 minutes, turning the meatballs occasionally, until the sauce has thickened and the turkey is cooked through with no pink remaining.
  6. 6
    Meanwhile, cook the wholemeal penne in boiling water until al dente, then drain.
  7. 7
    Stir the baby spinach into the tomato sauce until wilted, then toss through the drained penne and remove the pan from the heat.
  8. 8
    Tear over the fresh basil, fold it through the pasta and serve immediately with the remaining 5g parmesan scattered on top.
