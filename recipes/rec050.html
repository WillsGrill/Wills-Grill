Smoky Pork, Pepper & Butter Bean Casserole

With Spinach, Tomatoes & Rosemary

Ingredients

  • For the casserole

  • 500 g Pork Tenderloin, cut into bite-sized pieces.
  • 480 g Butter Beans, drained.
  • 400 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 each Red Pepper, sliced.
  • 1 each Yellow Pepper, sliced.
  • 1 each White Onion, sliced.
  • 200 g Baby Spinach
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 20 g Tomato Purée
  • 2 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Dried Rosemary
  • 0.25 tsp Chilli Flakes
  • 250 ml Low-Salt Chicken Stock
  • 1 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

  • To finish

  • 10 g Fresh Parsley, chopped.

Method

  1. 1
    Trim the pork tenderloin and cut it into bite-sized pieces, then slice the peppers and onion.
  2. 2
    Heat the rapeseed oil in a large casserole pan and brown the pork for four minutes, then transfer it to a plate.
  3. 3
    Add the onion and peppers to the same pan and cook for six minutes until softened.
  4. 4
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, tomato purée, smoked paprika, rosemary and chilli flakes for one minute.
  5. 5
    Add the chopped tomatoes, low-salt stock and drained butter beans and bring to a gentle simmer.
  6. 6
    Return the pork to the pan and simmer uncovered for 12 to 15 minutes until cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  7. 7
    Stir in the spinach a handful at a time until wilted and add a splash of water if the casserole is too thick.
  8. 8
    Taste, season with black pepper if needed and serve scattered with chopped parsley.
