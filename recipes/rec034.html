Fragrant Chicken, Chickpea & Courgette Curry

With Zesty Parsley Brown Rice

Ingredients

  • For the curry

  • 300 g Chicken Breast, cut into bite-sized pieces.
  • 240 g Chickpeas, drained and rinsed.
  • 1 each Courgette, cut into half-moons.
  • 1 each Shallots, finely chopped.
  • 200 ml Light Coconut Milk
  • 200 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 2 tsp Mild Curry Powder
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Ground Coriander
  • 0.5 tsp Turmeric
  • 0.25 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

  • For the rice

  • 120 g Brown Rice

  • To finish

  • 1 each Lime, zested and juiced.
  • 15 g Fresh Parsley, chopped.

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the brown rice according to the packet instructions until tender, then drain well.
  2. 2
    Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces, finely chop the shallot and cut the courgette into half-moons.
  3. 3
    Heat the rapeseed oil in a wide pan and cook the chicken, shallot and courgette for six minutes until the chicken is lightly golden.
  4. 4
    Add the Easy Garlic, curry powder, cumin, ground coriander, turmeric and cinnamon and stir for one minute until fragrant.
  5. 5
    Add the drained chickpeas, chopped tomatoes and light coconut milk and bring to a gentle simmer.
  6. 6
    Cook uncovered for 12 to 15 minutes until the sauce has thickened, the courgette is tender and the chicken is cooked through.
  7. 7
    Fold the lime zest, half the lime juice and most of the chopped parsley through the brown rice.
  8. 8
    Season the curry with the remaining lime juice and serve over the zesty rice with the remaining parsley.
