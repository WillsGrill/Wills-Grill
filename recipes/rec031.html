Pork Fillet Ragù

With Wholewheat Tagliatelle & Hidden Vegetables

Ingredients

  • For the pork ragù

  • 500 g Pork Tenderloin
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 150 g Carrots
  • 2 stick Celery
  • 200 g Mushrooms
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 30 g Tomato Purée
  • 800 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 cube Beef Stock
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano

  • For the pasta

  • 280 g Wholewheat Tagliatelle

  • To finish

  • 10 g Fresh Basil
  • 20 g Parmesan

Method

  1. 1
    Trim the pork tenderloin, cut it into chunks and pulse briefly in a food processor until coarsely minced, taking care not to turn it into a paste.
  2. 2
    Heat the olive oil in a wide pan and cook the finely chopped onion, carrot, celery and mushrooms for eight minutes until soft and lightly coloured.
  3. 3
    Add the pork and cook over a medium-high heat for five minutes, breaking it up, until no pink remains.
  4. 4
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, tomato purée and dried oregano and cook for one minute.
  5. 5
    Add the chopped tomatoes, crumbled beef stock cube and 200ml water, then simmer uncovered for 15 to 20 minutes until rich and thick.
  6. 6
    Meanwhile, cook the wholewheat tagliatelle according to the packet instructions until al dente, reserving a mugful of pasta water.
  7. 7
    Toss the pasta with the ragù, adding a splash of reserved water if needed, and check the seasoning.
  8. 8
    Divide between warm bowls and finish with fresh basil and a light grating of Parmesan.
