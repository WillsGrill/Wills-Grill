BBQ Salmon with Spanish Grains

With Tenderstem Broccoli, Mange Tout & Lemon

Ingredients

  • For the salmon

  • 2 fillet Salmon Fillet
  • 0.5 each Lemon
  • 5 g Fresh Dill
  • 0.5 tsp Black Pepper

  • For the Spanish grains

  • 150 g Spanish Grains
  • 0.5 each Lemon

  • For the vegetables

  • 160 g Tenderstem Broccoli
  • 140 g Mange Tout
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 0.25 tsp Sea Salt
  • 0.5 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the wholegrain Spanish grains according to the packet instructions and keep them warm.
  2. 2
    Toss the tenderstem broccoli and mange tout with extra virgin olive oil, Easy Garlic, smoked paprika, salt and pepper.
  3. 3
    Spread the vegetables over a lined baking tray and roast until just tender and lightly charred.
  4. 4
    Season the salmon with black pepper and place it on the tray beside the vegetables.
  5. 5
    Roast until the salmon flakes easily and the vegetables are browned at the edges.
  6. 6
    Squeeze over fresh lemon juice and sprinkle with chopped dill.
  7. 7
    Fluff the grains and fold through the lemon zest.
  8. 8
    Serve the salmon over the Spanish grains with the vegetables alongside.
