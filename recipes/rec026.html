Teriyaki Pork Tenderloin Stir-Fry

With Brown Rice, Broccoli & Mange Tout

Ingredients

  • For the teriyaki pork

  • 300 g Pork Tenderloin
  • 160 g Broccoli
  • 100 g Mange Tout
  • 1 each Red Pepper
  • 2 each Spring Onions
  • 2 tbsp Low-Salt Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tbsp Rice Vinegar
  • 2 tsp Easy Ginger
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tsp Cornflour
  • 1 tsp Sesame Oil

  • For the rice

  • 120 g Brown Rice

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the brown rice according to the packet instructions, then drain and keep warm.
  2. 2
    Whisk the low-salt soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, Easy Ginger, Easy Garlic, cornflour and two tablespoons of water into a smooth teriyaki sauce.
  3. 3
    Trim the pork tenderloin and slice it thinly across the grain so it cooks quickly.
  4. 4
    Heat half the sesame oil in a large non-stick wok and stir-fry the pork over a high heat until browned and just cooked through, then transfer to a plate.
  5. 5
    Add the remaining sesame oil, broccoli, sliced red pepper and mange tout and stir-fry for four to five minutes, adding a splash of water to help the broccoli steam.
  6. 6
    Return the pork to the wok and pour in the teriyaki sauce.
  7. 7
    Toss everything for one to two minutes until glossy and thickened, checking that the pork is piping hot.
  8. 8
    Divide the brown rice between bowls, top with the stir-fry and finish with sliced spring onions.
