Mediterranean Cod & Chickpea Traybake

With Courgette, Tomatoes, Olives & Lemon

Ingredients

  • 2 fillet Cod Fillet
  • 240 g Chickpeas
  • 250 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 each Courgette
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 1 each Red Pepper
  • 30 g Black Olives
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 10 g Fresh Parsley
  • 0.5 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Heat the oven to 200°C fan and line a large, shallow baking tray.
  2. 2
    Slice the courgette, red onion and pepper and add them to the tray with the tomatoes and drained chickpeas.
  3. 3
    Toss the vegetables and chickpeas with the olive oil, Easy Garlic, oregano, smoked paprika, lemon zest and black pepper.
  4. 4
    Roast for 15 minutes, turning the vegetables once.
  5. 5
    Pat the cod dry, place it among the vegetables and squeeze over half the lemon juice.
  6. 6
    Scatter the olives around the tray and return it to the oven for 10 to 12 minutes until the cod flakes easily.
  7. 7
    Squeeze over the remaining lemon juice and gently stir the tray juices through the chickpeas.
  8. 8
    Divide between two plates and finish with plenty of chopped parsley.
