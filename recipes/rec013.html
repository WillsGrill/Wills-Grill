Mediterranean Baked Salmon

With Roasted Vegetables & Wholewheat Couscous

Ingredients

  • 2 fillet Salmon Fillet
  • 1 each Courgette
  • 1 each Aubergine
  • 1 each Red Pepper
  • 120 g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 each Red Onion
  • 150 g Wholewheat Couscous
  • 1 each Lemon
  • 1 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 5 g Fresh Basil
  • 5 g Fresh Parsley
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 0.25 tsp Sea Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper

Method

  1. 1
    Prepare the wholewheat couscous in a separate bowl and fluff it with a fork once tender.
  2. 2
    Chop the courgette, aubergine, pepper, cherry tomatoes and red onion into roasting pieces.
  3. 3
    Toss the vegetables with extra virgin olive oil, oregano, salt and pepper.
  4. 4
    Roast the vegetables until softened and lightly caramelised.
  5. 5
    Season the salmon and place it on top of the tray for the final part of cooking.
  6. 6
    Bake until the salmon is just cooked through.
  7. 7
    Finish with lemon juice and chopped basil and parsley.
  8. 8
    Serve the salmon with the vegetables and couscous.
