Crispy Chicken Bibimbap

With Pak Choi, Carrot, Egg & Brown Rice

Ingredients

  • 300 g Chicken Breast
  • 120 g Brown Rice
  • 2 each Pak Choi
  • 150 g Carrots
  • 2 each Egg
  • 2 each Spring Onions
  • 1 tsp Easy Ginger
  • 1 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 2 tbsp Low-Salt Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 1 tbsp Rice Vinegar
  • 2 tsp Sesame Oil
  • 2 tsp Cornflour

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the brown rice according to the packet instructions, then drain and keep warm.
  2. 2
    Mix the low-salt soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, Easy Ginger and Easy Garlic to make a quick alternative to ketjap manis.
  3. 3
    Slice the chicken into thin strips, coat with the cornflour and half the sauce, and set aside for five minutes.
  4. 4
    Heat one teaspoon of sesame oil in a non-stick pan and cook the chicken until crisp at the edges and completely cooked through.
  5. 5
    Remove the chicken, then stir-fry the shredded carrot and sliced pak choi in the remaining sesame oil until just tender.
  6. 6
    Fry the eggs in the same pan until the whites are set and the yolks remain soft, or cook to your preference.
  7. 7
    Divide the rice between bowls and arrange the chicken, carrot and pak choi separately over the top.
  8. 8
    Add an egg to each bowl, drizzle with the remaining sauce and finish with sliced spring onions.
