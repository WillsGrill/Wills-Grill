Turkey Keema with Peas & Spinach

With Brown Rice, Tomatoes & Warming Spices

Ingredients

  • For the turkey keema

  • 500 g Turkey Mince
  • 400 g Chopped Tomatoes
  • 200 g Frozen Peas
  • 200 g Baby Spinach
  • 1 each White Onion
  • 2 tsp Easy Garlic
  • 2 tsp Easy Ginger
  • 2 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Ground Coriander
  • 0.5 tsp Turmeric
  • 0.25 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 150 ml Low-Salt Chicken Stock
  • 1 tbsp Rapeseed Oil

  • For the rice

  • 240 g Brown Rice

  • To finish

  • 1 each Lime
  • 15 g Fresh Coriander

Method

  1. 1
    Cook the brown rice according to the packet instructions, then drain and keep warm.
  2. 2
    Heat the rapeseed oil in a large frying pan and soften the finely diced onion for five minutes.
  3. 3
    Add the turkey mince and cook for six minutes, breaking it into small pieces until lightly browned.
  4. 4
    Stir in the Easy Garlic, Easy Ginger, cumin, coriander, turmeric and cayenne for one minute.
  5. 5
    Add the chopped tomatoes and low-salt stock and simmer uncovered for 10 minutes.
  6. 6
    Stir in the peas and cook for three minutes, then add the spinach a handful at a time until wilted.
  7. 7
    Squeeze in the lime juice and stir through most of the chopped coriander.
  8. 8
    Serve the turkey keema with the brown rice and finish with the remaining coriander.
